Welcome to the Brain Cleaning in OSA Research Study


We invite you to participate in a brand-new research study looking at the effects of CPAP on Dementia and Brain Health during both periods of sleep and wakefulness.

We will measure various aspects of your sleep over a 24-hr period and analyse blood markers for Dementia

You may be eligible to participate if you are

35 - 65 yrs old
Live in Sydney
Use a CPAP machine regulary all night

If eligible you will need to come into the Woolcock in glebe and stay overnight in one of our sleep study suites on two occasions

Participants will be reimbursed $450 for their time and receive a free OSA assessment

To determine if you are eligible, you will be asked a series of short questions (5 minutes). You will know immediately if you are eligible to participate
