Welcome to the Brain Cleaning in OSA Research Study

We invite you to participate in a brand-new research study looking at the effects of CPAP on Dementia and Brain Health during both periods of sleep and wakefulness.

We will measure various aspects of your sleep over a 24-hr period and analyse blood markers for Dementia

You may be eligible to participate if you are

35 - 65 yrs old
Currently live in Australia
Have difficulties staying and falling asleep

You may be eligible to participate if you are

  • Older than 18 years of age.
  • Currently experiencing chronic low back pain (low back pain lasting a period of 3 months or more).

Please click the button below to access the participant information statement and consent form. You must read them and provide consent before gaining access to the survey.
