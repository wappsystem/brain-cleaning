Welcome to the Brain Cleaning in OSA Research Study at the Woolcock Institute of Medical Research.

Sleep is a time when the brain rests and cleans itself of dementia-causing toxins. We are looking for people to participate in a brand-new research study to learn how obstructive sleep apnoea affects brain cleaning during sleep.

We will measure how your sleep affects markers of dementia in your blood over a 24hr period.
